Kelly Sacked after Head-Butt Scandal

Kelly was appointed in January of this year

After a hearing with the CCC on Monday last, Castletown have terminated both Paddy Kelly and Steven Creighan's contracts with immediate effect. The Monaghan managerial duo had only been at the club four months.
